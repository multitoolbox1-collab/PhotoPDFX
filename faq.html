Frequently Asked Questions

1. Is PhotoPDFX free?
Yes, it’s completely free with no registration required.

2. Are my files safe?
Yes, all conversions happen in your browser — no data is uploaded to any server.

3. Can I use it on mobile?
Yes, PhotoPDFX is 100% mobile-friendly and responsive.

4. What formats do you support?
We support JPG, PNG, GIF, WEBP for image-to-PDF and PDF to PNG/JPEG/WebP conversion.
