About PicToPDF

PicToPDF is a free online tool that allows users to convert their images to PDF format quickly and securely. Our mission is to provide a simple, user-friendly solution for converting images to PDF without compromising on privacy.

Our Story

Founded in 2023, PicToPDF was created to solve a common problem: the need to convert multiple images into a single PDF document without installing software or compromising privacy.

How It Works

Our tool uses client-side processing, which means your images are converted directly in your browser. They are never uploaded to our servers, ensuring complete privacy and security.
