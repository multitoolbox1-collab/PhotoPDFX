About PicToPDF

PicToPDF is a free online tool that allows users to convert their images to PDF format quickly and securely. Our mission is to provide a simple, user-friendly solution for converting images to PDF without compromising on privacy.

Our Story

Founded in 2023, PicToPDF was created to solve a common problem: the need to convert multiple images into a single PDF document without installing software or compromising privacy. We believe that simple tools should be accessible to everyone.

How It Works

Our tool uses client-side processing, which means your images are converted directly in your browser. They are never uploaded to our servers, ensuring complete privacy and security. We use modern web technologies to provide a fast and reliable conversion experience.

Features

  • 100% free to use with no hidden costs
  • No registration or account creation required
  • Secure client-side processing
  • Support for multiple image formats (JPG, PNG, GIF, WEBP)
  • Mobile-friendly interface
  • No file size limitations
  • Fast conversion speed
